Comments? I love comments. Contact me and I'll post insightful commentary and criticism. There is, however, a 140 minimum character requirement for all comments, unless you submit something truly beautiful, concise, and koan like. This stipulation is just a polite way of saying I don't plan on publishing "Kewl that's awesome bro", or "Your mother wears army boots". There is no automated form that a spam bot can submit to and cause tedium on my part. If there is no comment form, how will you comment? Well if your comment is in fact thoughtful, then you also have the brain power to find one of the multitude of communications channels I use and ping me. I am not a recluse and can be found blathering out on those social interwebs. Think outside of the box dude, answering machine messages will be accepted as a form of blog comment submission. Maybe you will get some gump satisfaction out of making me transcribe your inebriated rant, which does in fact touch on some important points.
